A Member of Distribution Officers

Sean and I are distribution officers this year and we are eager to see the new changes in TYR’s distribution efficiency. Based on my past experience, I understand it’s particularly hard to push the year-2 to wrap magazines and distribute them. So to comfort them and make them feel good, Sean and I have been keeping them company to wrap the magazines and guide them in details.

When they said they were busy with their works, Sean and I, of course, not only called others to help, but we also do the job ourselves. I even helped them apply for the cheque beforehand in case some of the reporters claimed that they are short of money. These are something the former distribution officers failed to do for us. However, we’re sorry that we only sent issue 5 out temporarily due to the incompletion of publication; however, we will be in hurry to start our engine again as long as the issues are ready.
